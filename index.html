Our brand

At City Index, we celebrate the craft of our traders. We celebrate their ability to push boundaries, explore new markets and master new skills with a sense of quiet confidence. And we appreciate their determination not to shy away from challenges, but embrace them. Ultimately, we are guided by a core belief – that we only succeed when our traders succeed.

So no matter where you are on your trading journey, City Index is here to put you in the driver’s seat of your own success. We understand that although you can never predict the markets, with discipline and insight and a dependable platform, you can achieve great things.

So just like our traders, we’ve spent over 35 years navigating volatile markets and enhancing our technology and our service singularly focused on helping our clients achieve – for every move they make.

Essence

Invested in your success

Insight

Success is achieved over a lifetime, not in one moment. Traders need a partner who is truly invested in their trading journey so they can become the best trader they can be.

Positioning

We believe in constant improvement and always striving to achieve more. So we exist to empower traders to make the most out of every trade because their success is our success.

The Logo

Our logo

Our logo is a straight forward and accessible wordmarque with rounded edges to provide a friendlier tone to the capital letters.

Use the logo artwork file when creating communications. The Logo should only appear using the system colour palette.

Clear space

To make sure our logo always remains prominent and legible, we have defined an area of clear space equivalent to the height of our wordmarques X. This area should be kept free from text, busy patterns and backgrounds, other logos or other visual distractions.

Please note that this page indicates the absolute minimum clear space. Where possible, more should be used.

Logo positioning

In most layouts, the logo should be aligned to the margin corners. Preference on corner should be decided by white space available in the design.

In cases where the logo is the main focus of the page it may be centred.

Minimum scale

As well as using clear space, we can also keep our logo legible by ensuring it is never reproduced smaller than the minimum sizes outlined opposite.

Note that these are absolute minimum sizes, and that it is recommended to use the logos bigger.

Recommended scale

On every application, we must ensure that the logo strikes a balance on scale. It must be large enough to clearly brand the application, but subtle enough to avoid undermining our trusted aesthetic.

Exception to the rule is for social media in which the CI element is recommended.

Please note that these guidelines are for the most common formats only. For extreme aspect ratios or small formats, exercise your best judgement.

Partnerships

Aligning partnership logos should follow the clear space rules. The logos should be separated by a round cap keyline in light grey, which is 2 px on screen, or 1 pt in print.
